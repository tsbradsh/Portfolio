PROJECT: "Interactive Multimedia Website for When You Sleep (Band)"
TECH STACK: HTML, CSS, JS, Three.js, Adobe CC

BACKGROUND:

In order to best meet the client's needs, I set out to build a web presence that embodied our dreamlike, digital-analog aesthetic. The client needed a site that combined visual storytelling, motion graphics, and functionality.

PROBLEM:

The client needed a fully responsive, self-managed platform that could handle multiple creative needs: show listings, music streaming, merchandise, and a visual brand rooted in glitchy, surreal identity.

PROCESS:

I designed and coded a custom, fully responsive site from scratch using HTML/CSS/JS. The design was based on a dark dreamscape theme, supported by an animated logo which I built using Adobe Creative Cloud software. I integrated a feed for show updates, embedded EP streaming, simulated scroll-based motion blur effects, and linked to an existing storefront.

RESULTS:

The website became the central hub for the client's launch and identity. The project demonstrated my ability to blend motion design, front-end development, and brand storytelling into a seamless user experience.

PROJECT: "AI Music Collaboration Tool (WIP)"
TECH STACK: Python, Magenta, MusicGen, Transformers

BACKGROUND:

As part of a self-directed exploration into AI, I began designing a tool that supports human musicianship rather than replacing it. The goal: build a local-first AI assistant trained on personal music styles to enable faster, deeper creative flow.

PROBLEM:

Many AI music tools feel generic and disconnected from the art/artist. I wanted something that learned alongside me, adapting to my personal voice/playing style and genre preferences. Privacy and local use were also key.

PROCESS:

I started by testing Magenta, MusicGen, and open-source transformer models in a local environment. I mapped out a multi-phase development plan that includes user onboarding and dataset training, song sketching with melody + lyric generation based on past projects, and visual feedback for iteration.

RESULTS:

Early prototype scripts can generate raw MIDI and text data tailored to my past writing. The tool has already shaped my thinking about AI's role in songwriting and the potential for human-AI co-creation rooted in an individual identity.

PROJECT: "Small-Business Web Design for Red Clover Sugar Studio"
TECH STACK: HTML, CSS, JS, Git, UX Strategy

BACKGROUND:

Red Clover Sugar Studio needed a brand-aligned online presence to educate clients and establish credibility in the wellness space.

PROBLEM:

Without an existing web presence, the studio lacked a place to explain their process, offer pre-/post-care information, and connect with potential clients.

PROCESS:

I planned the project through wireframes and iterative development utilizing Git for version control. Key focuses included a minimalist layout emphasizing calm, informational sections, and a polished contact form.

RESULTS:

The site serves as Red Clover's first professional digital presence, providing a polished platform for future e-commerce or booking integrations.
